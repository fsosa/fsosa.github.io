About

Solar Surveying, LLC is a professional consulting surveying firm established in 2018 in Houston, Texas.

Our firm offers land surveying services for projects in the areas of land development, transportation, drainage, and construction. Our mission is to provide the highest quality in surveying services and deliver products to all our clients that meet or exceed the technical and performance requirements. Utilizing state of the art equipment enables us to efficiently and accurately complete very complex projects with no delays.
